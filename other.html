A place for projects and ideas.

One of the most fascinating phenomena offered by the new technologies is the re-signification of the concept of 'place', on the one hand favoured by the generation of new reticular forms of interaction capable of reducing the spatial dimensions linked to physical contiguity and weakening any perception of 'border' (Global Village and Space of Flows), and on the other offered by the possibility of exploiting new strategies of representation and archiving of the attributes intimately linked to a territory: histories, geographies, knowledge. Although today a 'place' should be considered in its dichotomous 'real-virtual' extension, it continues to preserve in both dimensions the character of a 'space of crossing', a repository of traces of the past and convergence of heterogeneous information flows belonging to the culture of individual and collective memory.
