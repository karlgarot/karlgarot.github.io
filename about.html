Me at work with a laser touch projector.


I'm an engineer, designer and artist, with a special affection for the metamedium. Held a master's degree in Cinema and Media Engineering from Politecnico di Torino, I work developing multi-channel interactive systems and immersive experiences for exhibitions, museums and independent events, exploring new technologies.
Concurrently, I conduct a multidisciplinary academic and artistic research straddling technologies and territories - in particular rural and mountainous ones, investigating how new media can theorize and put into practice new ways of thinking about landscapes, cultures and communities. My areas of interest includes computer graphics, parametric design, XR, image processing, physical computing, tangible and natural user interfaces prototyping, all of them united by an open and participatory approach oriented towards practices of do it yourself and with others.
